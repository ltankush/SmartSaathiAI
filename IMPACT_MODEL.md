## Key Differentiator: Agentic AI

Unlike typical chatbots that only generate text, SmartSaathiAI's agent **actually calls real calculation functions** and returns exact numbers:

- Tax comparison: Real slab-wise computation, not LLM estimation
- SIP projection: Exact compound interest formula, not approximation
- FIRE planning: Inflation-adjusted corpus with milestone tracking
- BMS Score: Deterministic weighted scoring, not AI hallucination

This ensures **100% accuracy in financial calculations** while maintaining the warmth and accessibility of a Hinglish-speaking AI mentor.
